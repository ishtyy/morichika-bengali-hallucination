# Gemma model bindings

Base-model binaries are not committed to GitHub. The Kaggle notebooks bind them immutably through their source identifiers and local hash gates.

- Gemma 4 E4B QAT UD-Q4 GGUF: 4,215,695,776 bytes, SHA-256 `df0fd4ee…`; distributed under the applicable Gemma terms through the private Kaggle model dataset.
- MORICHIKA step-10 LoRA GGUF: 69,799,104 bytes, SHA-256 `ebef35b50d943ccb94a0749993b20aa408331d0615a2639525be263cdfe8a8e2`.
- MORICHIKA native step-10 adapter: 139,602,808 bytes, SHA-256 `191cea1e…`.

The LoRA artifacts are internal research checkpoints and have not passed the promotion gate. Their inclusion in this private repository is for reproducibility, not a quality claim or public redistribution grant.
